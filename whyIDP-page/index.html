Why do I need an IDP?

Discover the key benefits and legal reasons for carrying an International Driving Permit.

Essential Travel Companion

An International Driving Permit (IDP) serves as an official companion to your valid driver's license, allowing you to drive internationally with ease. It translates your license information into multiple languages, helping you communicate with local authorities and rental car agencies. The IDP acts as a bridge between your home country license and helps bridge communication gaps, especially in non-English-speaking countries.

Applied in over 180 countries, obtaining one is straightforward and quick. Applying online takes only a few minutes.

Application Requirements

Applicants must be 18 years or older possess a valid driving license, and no police field are required.

Printed IDP Booklet

The IDP booklet, produced in printed format, encompasses the driver's license information submitted during the online application process.

It comprises a total of 10 pages, incorporating:

  • Validity period
  • List of countries traditionally accepting the legal IDP
  • Information for driving with the IDP (available in 10 languages)
  • Your photograph
  • Your signature
  • Your first and surnames
  • Your country of birth
  • Your date of birth
  • Your country of residence

The maximum validation period offered for the IDP is 1 year. You can utilize the QR code to access your My Order page, allowing you to verify the validity and specifics of your order. The printed IDP will be delivered to your specified address, and the estimated delivery time may vary based on your selected delivery method, ranging from 3 to 10 working days.

IDP Booklet (Digital)

The Digital IDP Booklet is a PDF version of your Legal IDP Booklet. Legal recognition and acceptance may vary by country.

The Digital IDP Booklet is available immediately after your order is processed. You can download it from the "My Order" section. However, it is important to note that some countries, such as the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and Saudi Arabia, do not recognize digital versions.

This card serves as a tangible supplementary identification document, providing essential personal information akin to the details included in your IDP Booklet. Designed for convenience, it comes in a compact, wallet-friendly size, ideal for easy storage during your travels.
