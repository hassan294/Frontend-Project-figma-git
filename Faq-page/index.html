01 How do I receive/access my IDP? +
Your International Driving Permit will be delivered to your specified address within 5-7 business days. You'll also receive a digital copy via email for immediate use.
02 Is this government-issued? +
IDPs are essentially a translation document. It is not a government-issued document and an official driver's license. It only serves as a complementary document that translates your original license, thus allowing you to drive overseas.
03 Where can I use this IDP? +
Your IDP is valid in over 150 countries worldwide. Check our countries page for a complete list of supported destinations.
04 Is Digital IDP accepted everywhere? +
While digital IDPs are increasingly accepted, we recommend carrying both digital and physical copies for maximum compatibility across different countries and rental agencies.
05 Which countries do not accept your IDP? +
Most countries accept our IDP, but some have specific requirements. Please check our countries page or contact us for specific destination information.
06 How do I verify the validity of my IDP? +
You can verify your IDP through our online verification system using your permit number and personal details provided during application.
07 Special Considerations for Japan. What does this mean? +
Japan has specific requirements for IDPs and only accepts permits from certain authorized organizations. Please contact us for Japan-specific guidance.
08 Why can't I select the USA as a Country of Residence? +
US residents have specific IDP requirements and must apply through authorized US organizations. Please contact us for guidance on US applications.
09 Do you offer any refunds or guarantees? +
We offer a satisfaction guarantee and refund policy. Please review our terms and conditions or contact customer service for specific refund requests.
