Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick, clear answers to the most common questions about our services, process, and support — all in one place.

What is an International Driving Permit (IDP)?
An IDP is a translation of your domestic driver's license and allows you to drive legally in many countries worldwide.
Is an IDP a replacement for my driver's license?
No. It must be accompanied by your original, valid license. The IDP validates your right to drive abroad alongside your home license.
How long is the IDP valid for?
Validity depends on the option you choose (3, 5, or 10 years) from the date of issue.
How quickly can I get my IDP?
Digital versions are typically issued within minutes. Printed versions are shipped to your address.
What documents do I need?
A valid driver's license and a passport-style photo are required to apply.
